# hidden-space-finder

Part of the Fridge Combo project. A minimalist tool to reveal invisible characters that break your code and layout.

## 1 Tool / 1 Action

Paste your text, and instantly see what's hidden. Full-width spaces (IDSP) and Tabs (TAB) are highlighted with colored tags in real-time. No more guessing why your code doesn't work or why your design is misaligned.

## Clean & Professional

Use the "Clean & Copy" button to automatically convert full-width spaces into standard half-width spaces and remove unnecessary tabs. Get a perfectly "clean" version of your text back into your clipboard in one tap.
